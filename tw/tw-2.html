Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Inter Miami and the Argentina national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Al Nassr and captains the Portugal national team. Known for his incredible goal-scoring ability.

Pelé

Pelé was a Brazilian professional footballer who played as a forward. Regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, he won three FIFA World Cups with Brazil.

Diego Maradona

Diego Maradona was an Argentine professional football player and manager. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history, known for his dribbling ability.

Zinedine Zidane

Zinedine Zidane is a French former professional football player and manager. Known for his elegance and technical skills, he led France to World Cup victory in 1998.

Johan Cruyff

Johan Cruyff was a Dutch professional footballer and manager. He created the "Cruyff Turn" and was a proponent of "Total Football," revolutionizing modern soccer.

Ronaldo Nazário

Ronaldo Nazário, known as "Il Fenomeno," is a Brazilian former professional footballer who played as a striker. He won two Ballon d'Or awards and two World Cups.

Franz Beckenbauer

Franz Beckenbauer is a German former professional footballer and manager. Nicknamed "Der Kaiser," he is regarded as one of the greatest defenders in football history.
