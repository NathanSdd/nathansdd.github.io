Hook Head Lighthouse

Standing Guard for Over 800 Years

Guided Lighthouse Tours

Climb the 115 steps to the top of this historic beacon and be rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of the Irish Sea and the Waterford coastline. Our knowledgeable guides will share fascinating stories from the lighthouse's long and storied past.

Tours run daily and take you through the various levels of the tower, explaining the changing technology from the original candlelight to the modern automated system that guides ships safely today.

The Visitor Centre & Exhibition

Explore our interactive exhibition located in the restored lightkeepers' cottages. Discover what life was like for the families who lived in this remote and dramatic location, maintaining the light through storms and calm.

The exhibition features historical artefacts, hands-on displays, and audiovisual presentations that bring the 800-year history of Hook Lighthouse to life, making it a fascinating visit for all ages.

Coastal Walks & Wildlife

The headland surrounding the lighthouse offers spectacular coastal walks with unrivaled views. Follow the paths along the dramatic cliffs and watch the powerful waves crash against the oldest operational lighthouse in Ireland.

Keep an eye out for local wildlife, including seals basking on the rocks below and a variety of seabirds soaring above. The area is a haven for nature lovers and photographers seeking that perfect shot.
