Sligo Animal Rescue

Rescuing, Rehabilitating, and Rehoming since 2010

Animal Rescue & Emergency Response

Our dedicated team is on call to respond to reports of abandoned, injured, or neglected animals across Sligo and the surrounding areas. We work closely with local authorities to ensure every animal gets the urgent care they need.

If you find an animal in distress, please contact our emergency helpline. We provide a safe and secure pickup service to bring the animal to our state-of-the-art rescue centre for immediate veterinary attention.

Veterinary Care & Rehabilitation

Every animal that comes into our care receives a full health check from our partner veterinarians. We provide all necessary treatments, vaccinations, microchipping, and spay/neuter services to prepare them for a healthy new life.

Our rehabilitation program focuses on both physical and emotional healing. For animals that have experienced trauma, our volunteers provide gentle socialization and behavioural support to help them trust humans again.

Adoption Services

Finding loving, permanent homes for our animals is our ultimate goal. We have a thorough but straightforward adoption process to ensure each animal is matched with the perfect family for their needs.

Browse our online gallery of adorable pets waiting for their forever homes. From dogs and cats to rabbits and other small animals, we help you find a new best friend while giving a rescued animal a second chance at happiness.
